4.1 Refundable deposit: R 12,500.00 (equivalent to one month's rent)

11.2 Tenant liable for unpaid rent until new tenant found or lease expires.
11.3 Landlord may claim damages for breach including legal costs.

12.1 Landlord may inspect premises with 24 hours written notice during reasonable hours.

12.3 Final inspection shall occur within 3 days of termination.

14.1 Disputes shall first be addressed through negotiation.

14.3 Final recourse through Rental Housing Tribunal or courts.
14.4 This agreement is governed by the Rental Housing Act 50 of 1999 and South African law.
