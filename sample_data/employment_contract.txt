1. POSITION AND DUTIES

The Employee is appointed as Senior Software Developer and shall perform the following duties:
- Design and develop software applications
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams
- Maintain code quality and documentation
- Participate in code reviews and technical planning

2. COMMENCEMENT AND DURATION

This employment shall commence on 1 April 2024 and shall continue indefinitely subject to termination as provided in this agreement.

3. REMUNERATION

3.1 The Employee shall receive a monthly salary of R 45,000.00 (Forty-Five Thousand Rands), payable on the last working day of each month.

3.2 The salary shall be subject to annual review and may be increased at the Employer's discretion.

3.3 Annual bonus of up to R 50,000.00 may be awarded based on performance.

4. WORKING HOURS

The normal working hours shall be 40 hours per week, from Monday to Friday, 08:00 to 17:00, with one hour lunch break.

5. LEAVE ENTITLEMENT

5.1 Annual Leave: 21 working days per annum
5.2 Sick Leave: As per the Basic Conditions of Employment Act
5.3 Family Responsibility Leave: 3 days per annum

6. PROBATION PERIOD

The Employee shall be subject to a probationary period of 3 months from the commencement date. During this period, either party may terminate employment with one week's notice.

7. TERMINATION

7.1 After probation, either party may terminate this agreement with one month's written notice.

7.2 The Employer may terminate immediately for serious misconduct including but not limited to: theft, fraud, assault, gross negligence, or breach of confidentiality.

7.3 Payment in lieu of notice: The Employer may pay one month's salary instead of requiring notice period.

8. CONFIDENTIALITY

The Employee agrees to maintain strict confidentiality regarding all proprietary information, trade secrets, client data, and business strategies of the Employer, both during and after employment.

8.1 This obligation shall survive termination of employment indefinitely.

8.2 Breach of confidentiality may result in immediate dismissal and legal action for damages.

9. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

All work product, inventions, and intellectual property created during employment shall be the exclusive property of the Employer.

10. NON-COMPETE CLAUSE

For a period of 6 months following termination, the Employee shall not engage in any business that directly competes with the Employer within a 50km radius of Johannesburg.

11. LIABILITY AND INDEMNITY

The Employee shall indemnify the Employer against any loss or damage resulting from the Employee's negligence, wilful misconduct, or breach of this agreement.

12. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

12.1 Any disputes arising from this agreement shall first be addressed through internal grievance procedures.

12.2 Unresolved disputes may be referred to the Commission for Conciliation, Mediation and Arbitration (CCMA).

12.3 This agreement shall be governed by South African law and the Labour Relations Act 66 of 1995.

13. AMENDMENTS

No amendment to this agreement shall be valid unless reduced to writing and signed by both parties.

14. GENERAL PROVISIONS

14.1 The Employee must comply with all company policies and procedures.
14.2 Medical aid contribution: The Employer shall contribute R 2,500.00 monthly towards medical aid.
14.3 Pension fund contribution: The Employer shall contribute 8% of monthly salary.
